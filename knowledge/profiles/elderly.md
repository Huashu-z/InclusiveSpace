# Elderly Profile

Profile name: Elderly person.

Typical walking speed: 3.0 km/h.

Relevant comfort variables: stair, slope, unevenSurface, poorPavement, kerbsHigh, obstacle, trafficLight, station, wcDisabled, light.

Recommended variable values:
- stair: 0.5
- slope: 0.5
- unevenSurface: 0.5
- poorPavement: 0.5
- kerbsHigh: 0.5
- obstacle: 0.5
- trafficLight: 0.7
- station: 0.9
- wcDisabled: 0.9
- light: 0.8

Explanation: Elderly pedestrians may walk more slowly and may be more sensitive to stairs, slopes, uneven surfaces, poor pavement, high kerbs, obstacles, poor lighting, and lack of nearby supportive infrastructure.

Warnings or limitations: These values are deterministic defaults for CAT. They should be filtered by city availability before being applied.
